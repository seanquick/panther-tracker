Nutrition Strategies

Bulk Up (Muscle Growth)

The healthiest way for a teenager to gain muscle is through a combination of consistent resistance training (3-4 times a week), consuming a slight calorie surplus (300-500 extra calories daily) focusing on whole foods, and getting 8-10 hours of sleep for recovery.

Nutritional Strategy

  • • Increase Calories: 300–500 extra calories daily.
  • • Prioritize Protein: Aim for 1 gram per lb of bodyweight.
  • • Fuel with Carbs: Oats, brown rice, and potatoes.

Lean Down (Healthy Weight Loss)

To lose weight healthily, teens should focus on nutrient-dense foods rather than extreme calorie cutting.

Macro Breakdown

  • • Protein (10–30%): Target 1.2–1.5 g/kg of weight.
  • • Carbohydrates (40–60%): Focus on fiber-rich grains.
