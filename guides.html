Nutrition Strategies

The Bulk Phase

  • 🔥 Surplus: Eat 300-500 calories above maintenance.
  • 🥩 Protein: Aim for 1g of protein per lb of bodyweight.
  • 🍚 Carbs: Fuel your lifts with high-quality complex carbs.

The Lean Phase

  • 📉 Deficit: Drop 200-300 calories below maintenance.
  • 💧 Hydration: Drink 1 gallon of water daily to maintain metabolic rate.
  • 🥗 Volume: Use high-volume greens to feel full on lower calories.
