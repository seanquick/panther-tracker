Teen Muscle Growth

Focus on a consistent caloric surplus with a diet high in protein and complex carbohydrates.

Nutritional Strategies

  • PROTEIN: 30-35% of calories. Chicken, fish, eggs, dairy, beans.
  • CARBS: 40-50% of calories. Pasta, rice, oats, fruits.
  • FATS: 20-30% of calories. Nuts, seeds, avocados.
Consult a professional before making significant diet changes.

Healthy Weight Loss

Focus on sustainable habits. Prioritize whole foods, hydration, and at least 60 mins of daily activity.

Eating Habits

  • Balance Your Plate: Lean proteins, whole grains, and colorful veggies.
  • Reduce Sugar: Limit soda and highly processed snacks.
  • Eat Regular Meals: Don't skip breakfast.
  • Drink Water: Choose water over sugary beverages.

Lifestyle

  • Cardio & Strength: Combine jogging/soccer with push-ups/planks.
  • Limit Screens: Max 2 hours of non-school screen time.
  • Prioritize Sleep: Aim for 8–10 hours for metabolism support.
Focus on feeling strong and energetic, not just the number on the scale.
