Privacy & Data Policy

1. Data Ownership (SB29)

All student athlete records remain the property of the participating Educational Institution. RACKSTAT acts as a service provider and does not claim ownership over student data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect names, grade levels, and performance metrics (height, weight, strength logs) strictly for tracking and recruitment visualization.

4. Right to Deletion

Parents may request the permanent deletion of a student's record at any time via the school's athletic department.

Terms of Service

1. Acceptance of Risk

RACKSTAT is a data-tracking tool. Users acknowledge that athletic training carries inherent risks. RACKSTAT is not liable for injuries sustained during training.

2. Accuracy

Users agree to provide accurate metrics. Falsification of data for recruiting advantages may result in account termination.
